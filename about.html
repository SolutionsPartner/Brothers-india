🌿 Our Story

Building Trust Since 2019

Brothers India was established in 2019 with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide healthcare facilities across India with access to high-quality medical equipment and supplies. What started as a small venture in Ranchi has grown into a trusted name in the medical equipment industry.

With over 5+ years of experience, we have built strong relationships with manufacturers, suppliers, and most importantly, our customers. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer service has earned us a 4.3-star rating and an impressive 94% response rate.

Today, we serve hospitals, clinics, diagnostic centers, and healthcare facilities across Jharkhand and beyond, supplying everything from ECG papers and test kits to advanced hematology analyzers and medical reagents.
